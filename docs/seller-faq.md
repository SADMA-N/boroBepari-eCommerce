# Seller Portal FAQ

## Why is my KYC pending?
KYC verification typically takes 24-48 hours. Ensure all documents are clear and valid.

## My withdrawal is delayed. What should I do?
Withdrawals can take 3-5 business days. If delayed, check the payout status or contact support.

## How do I update bank details?
Go to `/seller/profile` → Bank Details tab to add or update accounts.

## How do I respond to RFQs faster?
Use templates and enable RFQ notifications in `/seller/profile` → Notification Settings.
