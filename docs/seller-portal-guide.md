# Seller Portal Guide

## Overview
This guide walks sellers through key workflows in the BoroBepari Seller Central: onboarding, listing products, managing orders, and payouts.

## Getting Started
- Register as a seller: `/seller/register`
- Complete KYC: `/seller/kyc`
- Access the dashboard: `/seller/dashboard`

## Products
- Add a new product: `/seller/products/add`
- Manage products: `/seller/products`
- Bulk import: Use the Bulk Import button and download templates.

## Orders
- View and manage orders: `/seller/orders`
- Confirm, process, and ship orders.
- Download invoices and packing slips.

## RFQs
- Respond to buyer RFQs: `/seller/rfqs`
- Send quotes and track acceptance.

## Analytics
- Business performance: `/seller/analytics`
- Monitor revenue, orders, RFQ response, and inventory insights.

## Payouts
- Manage payouts: `/seller/payouts`
- Track escrow and withdrawal history.

## Settings
- Update business profile: `/seller/profile`
- Configure notifications, security, and store settings.
